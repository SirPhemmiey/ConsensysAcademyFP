ETHER OPTIONS


Welcome to Ether Options, ERC20 compliant Ether calls! For demonstration purposes, we assume that one stablecoin is equivalent to one USD. Start by wrapping your ether.


Once you've successfully wrapped your ether, provide the Ether Options smart contract with custodial access to some of your WETH

Now that you've approved your WETH, you can mint some ERC20 options!

Approve your stablecoins so that you can exercise your options when they expire

Input the amount of stablecoin you'd like to spend to redeem your calls. Keep in mind that the strikeprice for these calls is 500 stablecoin, input values must be in multiples of 500 or else the contract will throw. Once the correct block height has been reached (migration block + 500), feel free to redeem your options by specifying the amount you'd like to redeem and clicking the "Redeem Option" button.
